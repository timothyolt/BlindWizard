1. Import SVG into blender
2. Delete frame curve
3. While the other curves are selected, press Tab (edit mode).
4. Scale it to uniform size by pressing A (select all), S (scale), 416 (size), and Enter (to commit)
5. Press Tab again (leave edit mode).
6. Press Alt-C (Convert curve to mesh).
7. Press R (Rotate), X (select X axis), -90 (amount), and Enter (commit).
8. Ctrl-S (Save) in the Icons folder.
9. Disable Materials, Animation and Rigging in Unity importer.
